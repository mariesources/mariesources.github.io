Running Log of Updates


Updates are being made to various sections of the website, due to the fast-paced nature of the COVID-19 effects on global supply and logitics. This means that from order to order, there may and will be minor differences. I want to make sure these are transparent. In addition to the updates being made in their pertinent sections, I will keep a running here of changes that have been made.


CATEGORY DATE CHANGES
Site April 21, 2020 Marie added this update page. Below were retroactively added because they are important enough to show when the changes occurred.
Shipping April 20, 2020 Included links to the ongoing COVD-19 service announcement pages for Fedex and DHL. Viewable on the Price/Shipping page.
Shipping April 17, 2020 First time ordering disposable surgical masks. These masks are properly sterilized and vacuum packed, intended for medical use. Because of this, learned that shipments containing products that are officially medical-use, there is a required 85.72 USD (converted from Chinese currency, hence the decimals) required for the shipment.  
Shipping April 17, 2020 The big three shipping carriers - Fedex, UPS, and DHL - now all requiring mandatory customs declaration paperwork for outgoing shipments containing protective wear/products.
Shipping April 13, 2020 DHL started requiring 70 USD customs declaration paperwork for all outgoing shipments that contain protective wear/products.
