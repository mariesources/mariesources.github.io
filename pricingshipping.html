IMPORTANT: READ all information below for all shipping cost and duration details that may impact your shipment. Updates are being made to this page all the time to reflect the current global logistics situation. Thank you for your understanding and patience.

General Overview

Our China agent ships daily via DHL, Fedex, or UPS. The local agent keeps up-to-date on the freight availability of each company. On average, we have heard updates every week. These shipping companies hold shipments in large warehouses, pending flights. As of April 6th, flights became restricted, meaning certain packages were with Fedex/DHL/UPS, waiting in the warehouse to begin shipping. While our agent will hand over packages to courier services within 1-2 days of payment, prioritizing whichever courier can ship out the fastest, the actual ship time is not within our control. I hope you can be patient.

Whenever you're given an estimated ship date, it is a combination of the following:

  • how soon the agent can package product and arrange for shipment
  • how quickly the courier company can arrange for packagers to leave on a flight
  • how quick of a delivery service was chosen

Shipping Costs, with Examples

IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT: As of April 17th, express shipping services out of China require 2 different kind of fees that may affect a given shipment:

  • 70 USD fee per shipment for customs declaration when the shipment contains protective wear. This includes face shields, KN95s, and regular disposable masks.
  • 85.72 USD per shipment for Medical customs declaration when the shipment contains official medical wear. This includes the Disposable Surgical Masks (sterilized and vacuum packed). 

A recent shipment of both KN95 masks and disposable surgical masks had both of these fees levied on the shipment.

Every shipment's shipping cost is calculated on the exact weight and dimensions of your package(s), which means I cannot offer an accurate shipping quote when you place an order request on this site. However, you can reference the previous orders below to get an idea.
