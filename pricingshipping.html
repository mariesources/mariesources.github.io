Order By the box
  • KN95s: 10/pkg
  • Surgical Masks: 50/box
  • Face shields: 5/pkg

All the packaging is designed to efficiently hold these multiples, and efficient packing means lower shipping fees. Once you order outside the multiple, there many be a lot of empty space in the shipping boxes, which you still have to pay for in shipping fees. Ordering other amounts is also fine.

Update on 4/5 - I was just informed that official customs paperwork may be required for large quantities; this depends on both country of entry and quantity. This will be clarified in an invoice from the agent before payment.

There are 2 payment options available - a simple PayPayl payment or a bank transfer. Paypal is faster, bank transfers take more time and may incur banking fees. Because you are paying the agent directly, there will be a digital invoice issued showing the agent's name, not mine. Also, at this time there is no way to issue a W-9.

Disclaimer - I am a procurement employee of a bigger e-commerce company in a different industry, and my specialty is China; I don’t take an order directly; I pass it onto the agent based in China and introduce you directly, to give you the quickest turnaround time. I hope this isn’t a problem.

I'm not officially request a processing fee, but I would appreciate any donation you could make to the sourcing effort. Any amount is appreciated and will enable to me to continue sourcing for those in need.

Last Updated: early April, 2020
Our China agent will ship via DHL, Fedex, or UPS. The local agent keeps up to do date on the freight availability of each company, which fluctures with the day. These shipping companies have large warehouses to hold orders pending flights out. As of 4/6, Fedex flights became limited, meaning certain packages were with Fedex, but waiting in the warehouse to begin shipping. While our agent will try to ship with whichever courier can ship out the fastest, the actual ship time is not within our control. I hope you'll be patient.

Whenever you're given an estimated ship date, it is a combination of the following:

  • how soon the agent can package product and arrange for shipment
  • how quickly the courier company can arrange for packagers to leave on a flight
  • how quick of a delivery service was chosen
Recent Case

Our recent order of 600 pcs had a 4-day production timeline and was shipped to Colorado via DHL in 3 working days. Shipping was approx. 65 USD for her order, but this clearly will fluctuate with the final weight of an order; shipping prices are also going up for such fast shipping out of China to the US. Order total + shipping fees would be communicated before actual shipping. Cheaper shipping may be possible but at a slower shipping time. It is possible that larger orders will ship in separate packages to avoid overly heavy boxes.
